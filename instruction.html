Benchmark

is a tool for learning how much you should lift in bulgarian bench press. The main thing that differs this tool from its predecessors is that you don't have to fill in the 1RM to get the other values.

Bulgarian what?

Bulgarian bench press is an exercise in which you construct your sets in the following way:

8 reps on 60% of 1RM

1 rep on 95% of 1RM

7 reps on 65% of 1RM

2 reps on 90% of 1RM

6 reps on 70% of 1RM

3 reps on 85% of 1RM

5 reps on 75% of 1RM

4 reps on 80% of 1RM

All you have to do is fill in how much you can lift in either of the fields, and the app will calculate the rest for you.
